Description

The smash burger, create hamburgers and smash them

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Mayonaise
  • Ketchup
  • Lettuce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Kraft Singles Cheese

Steps

  1. Make 1/4 lbs. beef balls
  2. Smash the burgers on a sizzling plate
  3. Add salt, garlic powder, onion powder and pepper
  4. Flip the burger once the first side is browned
  5. When the burger is about done, add a slice of cheese
  6. Prepare the hamburger
  7. Serve
