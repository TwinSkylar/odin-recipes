Description

Chili stew recipe for something quick and easy

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Brown Sugar
  • Chili Paste
  • Beef Broth
  • Kidney Beans
  • Green Peppers

Steps

  1. Brown the beef, remove from pan
  2. Cook the onions, green peppers and garlic
  3. Mix everything into a single pot
  4. Add spices to your tasting
  5. Slow cook for hours
  6. Let the chili rest before serving
