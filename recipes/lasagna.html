Description

This is just a traditional lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Brown Sugar
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Spices
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
